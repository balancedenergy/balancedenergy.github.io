Balanced Energy

Turbo "Improved"

Balanced Energy is a custom game mode that takes the "Turbo" mode and adjusts items, heroes and abilities to get a balanced, fun match with a shorter duration (~30 minutes).

The objective is to improve the "Turbo" casual mode of Dota 2 in order to make it much more enjoyable and... why not? Take the ideas and suggestions of people who play it to create new items, abilities and even heroes.
